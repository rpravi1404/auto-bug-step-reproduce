Privacy Policy for Auto Bug Steps Reproducer

Last updated: October 30, 2025

Auto Bug Steps Reproducer (“the Extension”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we handle data while you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or sensitive website data. All user interactions (clicks, inputs, selections, etc.) are processed locally within your browser and are not sent to any external servers.

2. Data Usage

The recorded interactions are used solely to display human-readable “Steps to Reproduce” within the extension interface. The information remains temporary and accessible only to you.

3. Data Storage

All captured data is stored temporarily in your browser memory (via Chrome’s local storage APIs). Once you close or stop recording, the data is automatically cleared.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use or connect to any third-party analytics tools, tracking systems, or cloud databases.

5. Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions (“activeTab”, “scripting”, and “storage”) strictly for performing its recording and display functionality.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out via the project’s GitHub repository or email at rpravi.in@gmail.com.
